About Us

We Provide Finest Patient's Care & Amenities

The NASL Medical Center operates as a Limited Liability Company (LLC) established in accordance with the Civil Code of the Republic of Tajikistan. We use state-of-the-art methods for diagnosis and treatment of all types of infertility.

  • Licensed Private Medical Activity
  • Comprehensive Infertility Treatment
  • Advanced Laser Technology

We Serve In Different Areas For Our Patients

Covering a comprehensive range of medical services organized into reproductive, diagnostic, and surgical categories.

IVF & ART

In-vitro fertilization, ICSI procedures, and assisted reproductive technologies.

Genetics

Diagnosis of hereditary diseases and genetic testing for parentage.

Lab Diagnostics

PCR testing, hormone panels, and blood biochemical diagnostics.

Surgery

Laparoscopy, hysteroscopy, and laser operations (CO2).

Ultrasound

Fetal screening, uterus, kidneys, thyroid and doppler ultrasound.

Gynecology

Full range of gynecological surgeries and IUD insertion/removal.

Andrology

Treatment of male infertility and sexually transmitted infections.

Cryopreservation

Storage of oocytes and embryos using Dewar Containers.
